### Week 25

4Rm.02 Read, with support, an increasing range of short, simple fiction and non-fiction texts with confidence and enjoyment.
4Wca.03 Plan, write, edit and proofread a short sequence of sentences in a paragraph, with support.
4Sc.02 Describe people, places and objects, and routine past and present actions and events, using a short sequence of sentences.
4Sor.01 Link, with support, a short sequence of simple sentences using a limited range of connectives.

### Unit 6-9 & Tenses Review

**Vocabulary Focus (Units 6-9):**
- **Going Places (Unit 6):** Transportation (ferry, tram, tuk-tuk), travel infrastructure (pier, metro station), and descriptive adjectives (huge, enormous, tiny).
- **Australia & Weather (Unit 7):** Extreme weather (tornado, blizzard, drought), geographical features (coral reef, coast, rainforest), and animal terminology (wings, turtles).
- **Nature Matters (Unit 8):** Environmental conservation (pollution, global warming, eco-friendly, recycle, reuse, biodegradable).
- **School's Out! (Unit 9):** Camping gear (tent, backpack, sleeping bag), outdoor activities (hike, campfire), and digital literacy (search engine, bookmark).

**Grammar Focus (Tenses Review):**
- **Tenses:** Present Simple, Present Continuous (including Future meaning), Past Continuous, Present Perfect, and Future Forms (will / be going to).
- **Sentence Structure:** Tag Questions, Defining Relative Clauses (who, which, that, where).
- **Modals & Expressions:** Offers & Invitations ("Would you like"), Obligation & Possibility (must, have to, may, might).
- **Prepositions:** Prepositions of Direction (into, out of, from, towards) and thematic prepositions (Like vs. About).
