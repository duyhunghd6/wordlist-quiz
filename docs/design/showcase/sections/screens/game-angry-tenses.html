Prototype Features

  • 4 Targets: Players now have to choose between 4 specific verb/preposition choices by hitting the respective grammatical pig. Hitting the correct one updates the sentence board and increases the score.
  • Timed Gameplay: A robust CSS/JS countdown timer runs automatically, applying urgency to the question reading and aiming.
  • Blank Filling: Once a target is destroyed, the blank line is vividly updated with the player's selected word, showing whether it is correct or incorrect.
