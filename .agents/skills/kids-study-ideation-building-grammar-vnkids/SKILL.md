## Core Principles & Knowledge

This skill focuses on mitigating Vietnamese L1 to English L2 negative transfer and aligning with the Cambridge Primary Curriculum.

1. **L1 Interference:** Vietnamese lacks copula ("be" verbs), articles, and inflectional tense markers. Address this via targeted scaffolding.
2. **Pedagogical Models:** Use Presentation-Practice-Production (PPP) for early stages and Task-Based Language Teaching (TBLT) for later stages. Use inductive discovery over deductive rules.
3. **Visual Syntax (Shape Coding):** Represent grammatical functions with shapes/colors (e.g., Red Oval = Subject, Blue Hexagon = Verb).
4. **Mobile Scaffolding:** Use visual, verbal, and procedural scaffolding.
5. **Gamification:** Integrate goal-oriented tasks, spaced repetition, and low-anxiety progression loops to foster engagement.

## Guidance to Write (Implementation)

When designing or building features, ensure you:

- **Design UI/UX for specific L1 errors:** Build specific "presence checks" and drag-and-drop puzzles that force the inclusion of missing elements like the "be" verb or article using Shape Coding placeholders.
- **Adopt an Inductive Approach:** Create tasks that allow students to "notice" patterns through visual enhancement and audio cues rather than explicit text rules.
- **Implement Multimodal Feedback:** Ensure immediate haptic/visual/auditory feedback for correct or incorrect actions (e.g., a "bump" when forcing an incorrect connection).
- **Incorporate Parental Features:** Include shared experience prompts or "Take-home" activities to connect digital learning with real-world parent-child interactions.

## Guidance to Verify (Quality Assurance)

To ensure the resulting app delivers the best study experience:

- **L1 Error Tracing:** Verify that the core mechanics actively intercept and remediate common Vietnamese errors (omitted copulas, missing articles, wrong tenses).
- **Cambridge Alignment:** Check that the grammar mechanics align with the progression map (Stage 1 to 6) specified in the research.
- **Scaffolding Check:** Validate that cues and hints are provided _early_ in the process (Visual and Verbal), prioritizing visual feedback over text-heavy instructions.
- **Cognitive Load Test:** Ensure the gamification elements do not overshadow the pedagogical goal (100% fun, 100% learning).
