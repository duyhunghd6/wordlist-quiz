## Core Principles & Knowledge

This skill focuses on the neurobiology of gamified learning and teaching the abstract concept of tenses to young learners.

1. **Pedagogical Frameworks:** PPP, TTT, ESA, and TBL. TPR (Total Physical Response) is crucial for preschoolers.
2. **Neurobiology of Play:** Capitalize on dopamine-reward loops via anticipation and instant feedback. Maintain the "Flow State" using Dynamic Difficulty Adjustment (DDA).
3. **Age-Specific UX Segmentation:**
   - Preschoolers (3-5): Reactive, multisensory, primal gestures, voiceovers.
   - Early Elementary (6-8): Larger touch targets (60-80pt), progress visualization ("Trophy Rooms").
   - Tweens (9-12): Sophisticated UI, branching narratives, invisible guardrails.
4. **Visualizing Time:** Use interactive timelines, time-travel mechanics, and branching causality narratives to teach perfect aspects and continuous tenses.
5. **Voice AI:** Use child-optimized Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) to provide real-time diagnostic feedback and fluency practice.

## Guidance to Write (Implementation)

When designing or building features, ensure you:

- **Implement Multisensory Feedback:** Every tap by a young learner must yield an immediate visual/auditory response.
- **Design for Dynamic Difficulty (DDA):** Ensure the app seamlessly adjusts the challenge based on learner success/failure to maintain the "Flow State".
- **Abstract Time Visually:** When teaching verb tenses, rely heavily on interactive visual timelines, "layering" tenses progressively rather than using text explanations.
- **Utilize Spaced Repetition (SRS):** Use SM2 algorithms to delay review of mastered concepts while quickly reviewing failed concepts.
- **Character-Driven UI:** Use a friendly mascot (VUI mentor) to deliver instructions conversationally instead of static text blocks.

## Guidance to Verify (Quality Assurance)

To ensure the resulting app delivers the best study experience:

- **Age Appropriateness:** Check UI element rules (e.g., no swiping/double-tapping for under 8s, touch targets >= 60pt).
- **Feedback Loop Test:** Verify that dopamine loops are activated instantly after an action (less than a second delay).
- **Tense Concept Check:** Validate that "-ing" continuous actions require the "be" verb auxiliary in the game mechanic.
- **SRS Validation:** Ensure the game loop does not allow "cramming" and actively enforces a spaced distribution of grammar topics.
